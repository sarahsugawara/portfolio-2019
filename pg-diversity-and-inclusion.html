Elevating a Celebration

P&G Diversity & Inclusion Awards

This particular year, P&G wanted to create a more sophisticated and special awards ceremony. The system would need to apply across an event program, award certificate, glass award, presentation, and animation transitions. My concept—eventually selected—uses the P&G phase mosaics intertwined with textural photography from the 5 global regions as outlined by P&G.

P&G's Diversity and Inclusion department uses blue and orange. Those colors created a consistent foundation for the photorealistic colors in nature, artifacts, and structural elements from around the world. Below is the program booklet for the event.

The same elements were adapted for a landscape PowerPoint presentation template that would accommodate teams of varying sizes, and team photos taken the day of the event to be inserted before presenting.

Winners were to be given a glass award, while finalists were to be given framed certificates. Working with P&G's vendor, I designed a simple pillar that utilizes the front and back face to create the visual depth found in the other event materials.
